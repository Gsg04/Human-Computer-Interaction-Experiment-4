Project Introduction

This project is part of the Human-Computer Interaction course at Shandong University. It explores people's planning, experiences, and reflections regarding the National Day holiday.

The study includes a semi-structured interview and a questionnaire to gather comprehensive insights into holiday behaviors and preferences.
